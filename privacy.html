# Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** July 17, 2026 NaijaTax enlighten ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our cross-platform application across Web, Android, iOS, macOS, Linux, and Windows environments (collectively, the "Application"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Application. ### 1. Information We Collect To provide accurate tax calculations, community interactions, and secure profile persistence, we collect several types of data: * **Authentication Data:** When you log in using Google or Facebook (Meta) authentication, we receive access to your public profile parameters, including your legal name, verified email address, and profile picture URL. * **Biometric Data Logs:** If you opt to enable biometric login (such as Fingerprint or Face ID) for quick access, this authentication is processed entirely locally on your device's native operating secure enclave. We do not store or transmit your biometric thumbprints or facial matrices to our external servers. * **Tax Simulation Inputs:** Any financial data points, salary inputs for PAYE calculations, or transactional values for VAT estimation are processed dynamically to return calculations. * **AI Assistant Queries:** Text inputs provided to the embedded AI Tax Assistant are processed to generate contextual educational responses regarding Nigerian tax policies. * **Community Forum Content:** Text, comments, or topics you explicitly publish inside the public community forums are retained on our servers to build the interactive feed. ### 2. How We Use Your Information We leverage the processed information exclusively to: * Authenticate and protect your account profile identity across platforms. * Execute immediate calculations for PAYE, VAT, and related Nigerian fiscal duties. * Train and power the interactive responses of the AI Tax Assistant. * Maintain, index, and organize the user-generated community message boards. * Support local offline storage workflows using embedded client-side databases (Isar/Hive) so you can access historical calculations without an active internet connection. ### 3. Data Storage and Third-Party Sub-Processors We utilize secure cloud infrastructure and database routing layers to manage backend states. We do not trade or sell user profiles to advertising networks. Your data interacts with the following technical platforms: * **Google Cloud Platform & Meta Developers Portal:** For secure OAuth customer handshakes. * **Supabase / PostgreSQL Frameworks:** For core cloud database storage and encrypted customer session routing. ### 4. User Data Deletion Instructions (Meta & Google Compliance) You retain total ownership over your profile records. You may request the absolute purging of your user credentials and historical forum interactions from our servers at any time. To initiate a permanent data deletion request, execute the following protocol: 1. Dispatch an email directly to our administrative desk at **support@naijataxenlighten.com**. 2. Input the exact phrase **Data Deletion Request** into the message subject field. 3. Detail the registration email address used during your Google or Facebook authentication workflow. Our technical team will process the query, purge all production profiles, and clear server database hooks associated with your user identifier within 7 business days. ### 5.
